James Boggs

James Boggs is a graduate student at the University of Michigan pursuing a Ph.D. in computer science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence. He works in the Soar Lab under Dr. John Laird, studying artificial intelligence through the lens of cognition and cognitive systems. His primary interest is better understanding how a symbolic cognitive architecture can use neural input systems more effectively.

Research Interests

  • Sensing in cognitive architectures: How can we enable a symbolic cognitive architecture to use high-dimensional, continuous sensory input such as vision.
  • Embodied cognition: What can cognitive agents gain from being embodied and how does cognition change when real-world affordances matter.
  • Emotional AI: How can we impart emotion and empathy in artificial agents, moving from artificial intelligence to artificial sapience.

Adding Vision to Soar

As a graduate student, James Boggs is currently working on adding a perceptual processing component to the Soar cognitive architecture. He is expanding the Spatial-Visual System of Soar to include a perceptual working memory (WM) component as well as a peceptual long-term memory (LTM), which will allow Soar agents to ground scene graphs and working memory elements to visual input.

Nochi, Ltd.

James Boggs is the Lead Developer for Nochi, Ltd., the game studio producing Sifting Thyme, a romance-focused, foodie-themed visual novel for Android and iOS. As the Lead Developer, James manages the company's other programmers as well as working on the more difficult development tasks and high-level implementation design decisions.
